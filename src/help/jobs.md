+JOBS

Staff-only listing of all open jobs.

SYNTAX
  +jobs

NOTES
  Staff only. Shows all open jobs visible to your role. **Superusers**
  see every bucket; other staff only see buckets they have access to.
  Use `+job/bucket <bucket>` to filter.

EXAMPLES
  +jobs         List all open jobs (what you have access to).

SEE ALSO: +help job, +help archive
