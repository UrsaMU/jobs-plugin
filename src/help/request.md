+REQUEST

Submit and manage your own requests to staff. Use **+requests**
or **+myjobs** to list your open requests.

SYNTAX
  +request[/<switch>] [<args>]

SWITCHES
  /create   Submit to a specific bucket.
  /comment  Add a comment to your request.
  /cancel   Cancel your own request.
  /addplayer  Add another player as a viewer.

EXAMPLES
  +request My Issue=Please help with X.
  +request 3
  +requests
  +myjobs

SEE ALSO: +help request/syntax, +help request/examples
